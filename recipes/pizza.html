Margherita Pizza

A Margherita pizza is a true classic and a delicious masterpiece that has stood the test of time. Picture a perfectly baked, thin and crispy crust, with a light and airy texture that's just the right amount of chewy. As you take a bite, the crust gives way to a luscious layer of bright red tomato sauce, bursting with the bold flavors of tangy ripe tomatoes, fragrant garlic, and a sprinkle of aromatic herbs.

Ingredients

For the base

  • 300g strong bread flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast (from a sachet or a tub)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling

For the tomato sauce

  • 100ml passata
  • handful fresh basil or 1 tsp dried
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed

For the topping

  • 125g ball mozzarella, sliced
  • handful grated or shaved parmasan (or vegetarian alternative)
  • handful of cherry tomatoes, halved

To finish

  • handful of basil leaves (optional)
